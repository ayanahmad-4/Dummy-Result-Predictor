Integral University — grading formula
How marks are calculated
Each subject has two components:
CA (Continuous Assessment) — out of 60
ESE (End Sem Exam) — out of 40
Total = CA + ESE — treated as % out of 100
SGPI formula (university rule)
SGPI = Σ(Ci × Gi) ÷ ΣCi
Ci = subject credit  ·  Gi = grade points earned  ·  F / AB = 0 pts
